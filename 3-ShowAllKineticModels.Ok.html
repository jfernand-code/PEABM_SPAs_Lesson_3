Model Equation
Tamiya$$\mu = \frac{\mu_{max} \cdot I_{av}}{I_k + I_{av}} - m$$
Van Oorshot$$\mu = \mu_{max} \cdot \left( 1 - e^{-\frac{I_{av}}{I_k}} \right) - m$$
Blackmann$$\mu = \frac{\mu_{max} \cdot I_{av}}{2 \cdot \alpha} \left[ 1 + \frac{\alpha}{I_{av}} - \sqrt{\left( 1 - \frac{\alpha}{I_{av}} \right)^2} \right] - m$$
Molina et al.$$\mu = \frac{\mu_{max} \cdot I_{av}^n}{I_k^n + I_{av}^n} - m$$
Bannister$$\mu = \frac{\mu_{max} \cdot I_{av}}{(I_k^n + I_{av}^n)^{1/n}} - m$$
Camacho-Rubio$$\mu = \frac{\mu_{max} \cdot I_{av}}{2 \cdot \alpha} \left[ 1 + \kappa + \frac{\alpha}{I_{av}} - \sqrt{\left( 1 - \kappa - \frac{\alpha}{I_{av}} \right)^2 + 4\kappa} \right] - m$$
